About the Department

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, established in 2020 with an initial student intake of 60, has witnessed remarkable growth over the years. The current intake has expanded to 180 students, reflecting the increasing demand for this cutting-edge field. Alongside, the faculty strength has also grown steadily to support the department's vision. With its promising trajectory, the student intake is expected to rise further in the coming years, paving the way for greater advancements and opportunities in AI and Data Science education.

Department Vision

To enable aspiring engineers to excel in computational and design skills, empowering them to meet the demands of industry, society, and the global environment with innovation and responsibility.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

    PEO 1: To provide graduates with a strong foundation in Computer Science and Engineering, along with Mathematics and Science fundamentals, enabling them to model, analyze, design, program, and implement with global competence and embrace lifelong learning.

    PEO 2: To prepare graduates with knowledge of recent technological advancements in programming, databases, compiler design, network security, and future technologies, enabling them to contribute effectively to research and development.

    PEO 3: To train graduates to pursue careers with high employability, entrepreneurship, or higher education by empowering them with critical decision-making skills, ethical values, and preparation for competitive examinations.

    PEO 4: To develop interpersonal skills and a sense of social responsibility, preparing graduates to become effective team members and leaders.

Program Outcomes (POs)

    PO1: Engineering knowledge: Apply mathematics, science, and engineering principles to solve complex engineering problems.

    PO2: Problem analysis: Identify, analyze, and formulate engineering problems based on research and mathematical principles.

    PO3: Design/development of solutions: Design solutions that meet public health, safety, societal, and environmental needs.

    PO4: Conduct investigations of complex problems: Use research-based knowledge and experiments to draw valid conclusions.

    PO5: Modern tool usage: Apply modern engineering tools for complex activities, understanding their limitations.

    PO6: The engineer and society: Assess societal, health, and legal responsibilities in professional engineering practices.

    PO7: Environment and sustainability: Understand the impact of engineering solutions in environmental and societal contexts, emphasizing sustainability.

    PO8: Ethics: Commit to professional ethics and responsibilities in engineering practices.

    PO9: Individual and team work: Function effectively as an individual and as part of diverse and multidisciplinary teams.

    PO10: Communication: Communicate effectively through reports, presentations, and instructions in engineering activities.

    PO11: Project management and finance: Apply engineering and management principles to lead and manage projects in multidisciplinary environments.

    PO12: Life-long learning: Recognize the importance of lifelong learning in adapting to technological changes.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

AI & DS

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science (DS) are revolutionary fields transforming the world through innovation and insight. By combining cutting-edge algorithms and data analysis, these fields provide solutions to real-world problems across industries.

Data Science

With applications ranging from healthcare to transportation, AI and DS are shaping the future of technology. The branch at VVIT empowers students with skills in machine learning, data processing, and AI development.

Applications

AI and Data Science are revolutionizing industries through diverse applications:

Personalized healthcare and disease prediction

Personalized healthcare and disease prediction use data analysis and AI to tailor medical treatment and forecast disease likelihood, improving patient outcomes and saving lives.

Fraud detection and risk management in finance.

Fraud detection and risk management in finance use machine learning, predictive analytics, and rule-based systems to identify and prevent fraudulent activities, mitigating credit, market, and operational risks.

Recommendation systems for e-commerce platforms

Recommendation systems suggest personalized products to customers based on their behavior, preferences, and purchase history, increasing sales and customer satisfaction.

Autonomous vehicles and route optimization

Autonomous vehicles use sensors, GPS, and algorithms to navigate and optimize routes, reducing travel time, fuel consumption, and emissions, while improving safety and traffic flow.

Job Opportunities

Pursuing AI and DS opens doors to exciting career paths:

Data Scientist


A Data Scientist collects, analyzes, and interprets complex data to gain insights, make informed decisions, and drive business growth using machine learning, statistics, and programming skills.

Machine Learning Engineer

A Machine Learning Engineer designs, develops, and deploys artificial intelligence and machine learning models to solve real-world problems, using programming languages like Python and TensorFlow.

AI Research Scientist


An AI Research Scientist explores and develops new artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms, models, and techniques to solve complex problems, advance the field, and drive innovation.

Natural Language Processing Specialist

A Natural Language Processing (NLP) Specialist designs and develops algorithms, models, and systems that enable computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language, such as chatbots and language translators.
